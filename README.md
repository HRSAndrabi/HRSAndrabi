## Hassan Andrabi

I'm Hassan Andrabi, a PhD student at the [Centre for Brain, Mind and Markets](https://www.unimelb.edu.au/cbmm) at the [University of Melbourne](https://www.unimelb.edu.au/).

I run experiments to study the role of markets in society, and the extent to which they promote socially preferable outcomes.

I have previously worked as a software engineer in population health simulation modelling.
